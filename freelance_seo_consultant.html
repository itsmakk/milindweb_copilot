How long does it take to see results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term process. Most businesses start seeing noticeable improvements in 2–3 months, with significant growth in 6–9 months depending on competition and niche.

Do you offer both SEO and paid advertising services?

Yes. I provide full-service digital marketing, including SEO, Google Ads (PPC), and Social Media Marketing for a balanced growth strategy.

Can you manage my business's social media accounts?

Absolutely. I create, optimize, and manage social media profiles on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter to boost engagement and brand awareness.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

I work with startups, small businesses, and growing companies across industries like retail, healthcare, education, real estate, and professional services.

Do you provide monthly performance reports?

Yes. I deliver detailed monthly reports showing improvements in rankings, website traffic, leads, and conversions so you can measure ROI.

How do Google Ads differ from SEO?

SEO brings long-term organic traffic, while Google Ads (PPC) generates instant visibility and leads. I often recommend combining both for maximum impact.

Is freelance digital marketing affordable compared to agencies?

Yes! As a freelancer, I offer affordable pricing without overhead agency costs, while still delivering professional-grade strategies tailored to your business.

Will you optimize my website for mobile and speed?

Definitely. As part of technical SEO, I ensure your website is fast, mobile-friendly, and user-optimized, which helps rankings and user experience.

Can you help with local SEO for my small business?

Yes. I specialize in Local SEO strategies, including Google Business Profile optimization, map listings, and local keyword targeting to attract nearby customers.
